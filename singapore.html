BUDDHA TOOTH RELIC TEMPLE AND MUSEUM

The Buddha Tooth Relic Temple in Singapore's Chinatown is a stunning temple housing a sacred Buddha tooth relic. It showcases Buddhist art and culture, attracting worshippers and visitors alike.

SENTOSA

Sentosa is a vibrant island resort in Singapore, famous for attractions like Universal Studios, S.E.A. Aquarium, and beautiful beaches. It's a top destination for entertainment, relaxation, and adventure, offering something for everyone.

NIGHT SAFARI

Singapore's Night Safari is the world's first nocturnal wildlife park, offering unique nighttime animal encounters. Visitors can explore via tram rides or walking trails to see over 100 species in their natural habitats.

MERLION PARK

Merlion Park in Singapore is home to the iconic Merlion statue, a symbol of the city featuring a lion's head and a fish's body. Located near Marina Bay, it offers stunning views and is a popular spot for visitors.
